Biography

I am a graduate student at Univerity of of Alberta. I am in my second-year of Master of Science in Electrical and Computer Engineering, and my major is in Communications. I am a research assistant under the supervision of Dr. Majid Khabbazian, and I am doing research on Wireless Sensor Networks (WSN). I am also involved in a project on smart monitoring of livestock using a wireless sensor network of embedded systems. Since continuing to learn new languages and development techniques are important to me, I started exploring object oriented languages like Java through my masterâ€™s program. With respect to the ever growing application of scripting languages and agile programming paradigm, I switched to Ruby and Rails so I could proceed toward web development. In this regard, I also started getting familiar with more modern front-end frameworks such as AngularJS.

I did my Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering at Ferdowsi University of Mashhad, and my major was in Electronics. In my undergraduate studies, I was focused on three main topics of:

  • Embedded Systems (design and coding)
  • Digital System Design (using VHDl and Verilog hardware programming)
  • Computer Architecture

More details on the main projects that I have done through my undergraduate and graduate studies are provided in the Projects section.
